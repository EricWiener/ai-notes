**What is the C2PA standard and what does it enable?**
C2PA is an open technical standard that allows publishers, companies, and others to embed metadata in media for verifying its origin and related information. C2PA isnâ€™t just for AI generated images - the same standard is also being adopted by camera manufacturers, news organizations, and others to certify the source and history (or provenance) of media content.

**How easy is it to remove C2PA Metadata?**
You can easily remove metadata by either taking a screenshot of a file or using a tool like [tone](https://github.com/sandreas/tone).
